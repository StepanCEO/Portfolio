## Freelancer Portfolio Concept

### Product
Personal portfolio site for a freelance creative who designs and builds polished digital products.

### Audience
Founders, small teams, and local businesses looking for a designer-developer to create landing pages, brand sites, and lightweight product marketing sites.

### Copy Lock
- Brand/name: Aleksa Morozov
- Navigation: Work, Services, About, Contact
- Primary CTAs: View projects, Book a call
- Hero headline: Freelance designer-developer for clean, high-converting websites.
- Hero body: I design and build portfolio sites, landing pages, and small product websites for people who want the work to feel calm, premium, and easy to trust.

### Section Order
1. Quiet header with brand, nav, and primary action.
2. Split hero with personal copy and a visual mosaic of project cards.
3. Services band with three clear offers.
4. Featured work section with case-study style project cards.
5. Process rail showing Discover, Design, Build, Launch.
6. Testimonial and availability block.
7. Simple pricing/engagement options.
8. Closing contact band and footer.

### Visual Direction
- Tone: editorial, handmade, confident, not corporate.
- Background character: warm stone field with subtle grain, clay, and muted teal gradients.
- Typography: Fraunces for headlines, Inter Tight for body and UI.
- Palette:
  - Ink `#1f2328`
  - Moss `#6b7c6a`
  - Clay `#d9c2ac`
  - Stone `#f3efe8`
  - Ink blue `#2e4a57`
  - Copper accent `#c56d4a`
- Containers: open layouts, bands, rails, and a few refined cards rather than a dense grid.
- Motion cues: slow float on project artwork and subtle reveal-like hover motion.

### Component Rules
- No hero eyebrow, badge, or pretitle above the headline.
- Keep the header minimal.
- Use project cards, service cards, and process cards sparingly and intentionally.
- Buttons should feel compact and editorial, with rounded pill geometry and crisp typography.
- Visuals should feel like handcrafted portfolio pieces rather than stock imagery.
- Public-facing proof should stay qualitative unless the user later supplies approved metrics.

### Interaction Baseline
- Project cards lift on hover and show a short label.
- Buttons have visible hover states.
- Layout must remain composed on mobile, with the project mosaic stacking under the hero copy.
